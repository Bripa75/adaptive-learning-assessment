Founder Story

Why we are building this together

👩‍⚕️

Co-Founder: Clinical and Student Support Perspective

One of the co-founders is a Licensed Speech Pathologist with extensive experience supporting students with diverse learning needs. Her work brings a deep understanding of communication, student support, and the real barriers many learners face in traditional assessment environments.

She also works in the Department of Education, which means this platform is being shaped by direct experience with how schools operate and what students actually need.

💻

Co-Founder: Platform Development Perspective

The second co-founder is focused on platform development and turning this vision into a real product. That means designing the website, shaping the user experience, and building a system that feels modern, supportive, and different from anything currently available.

Together, this creates a strong founder combination: one side grounded in student support and school-based reality, and the other focused on building the platform itself.

Mission

Assess more than just right or wrong

Our mission is to create a more supportive assessment platform for students with diverse learning needs — one that measures academic understanding, communication, social reasoning, life skills, and problem solving in a way that feels respectful and useful.

Why this matters

Traditional assessments leave too much out

Many existing assessments focus only on standard academic performance. They often miss how students process language, respond socially, handle real-world situations, and show what they know when given the right kind of support.
