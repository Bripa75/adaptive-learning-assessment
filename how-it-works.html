How It Works

Simple for families.
Powerful for understanding.

One assessment session. A clear picture of where your child is and what they can do — on their own terms.

01

Parent creates an account

Sign up in 2 minutes. Add your child's profile — name, grade, and any notes about their learning needs. One account can hold multiple children.

→
02

Choose category and level

Select a skill area — Math, Reading, Financial Literacy, Social, Safety, Time & Planning, or Decision-Making. Set the starting difficulty based on what you know about your child.

→
03

Student takes the assessment

One question at a time, untimed. Students can use the Listen button to hear the question read aloud, tap a Hint if they're stuck, or ask for step-by-step guidance.

04

Platform calculates results

Every answer, every hint used, every listen tap is recorded. The engine calculates a level, a score, and notes which supports helped — all automatically.

→
05

Parent and child see the report

A clear, plain-language report shows exactly what level was reached, what the student did well, and what they might focus on next. Both parent and child see the same report.

→
06

Download and share

Export the report as a PDF to share with an IEP team, therapist, or private tutor. Reassess after 30 days to track growth over time.

Built-In Features

Every student gets the right support

🔊

Read Aloud

Every question can be read aloud using the device's built-in text-to-speech. No extra app, no cost. Especially helpful for students with reading differences or auditory processing needs.

💡

Hint System

Optional hints reveal a guided clue for any question without giving away the answer. The platform records whether a hint was used, so it shows up in the report.

📋

Step-by-Step

Some questions include a step-by-step scaffold that breaks down how to approach the problem. Students can use it or skip it — the choice is theirs.

⏱️

Completely Untimed

There is no clock. No pressure. Students work at their own pace on every question. This is deliberate — timed tests measure anxiety, not ability.

📊

Four Adaptive Levels

Level 1 (Supported) through Level 4 (Advanced). The starting level is set by the parent. The result level reflects actual performance — the platform adjusts the final level up or down based on the score.

📄

PDF Export

Every report can be downloaded as a clean PDF. Share it with your child's IEP team before a meeting, send it to a therapist, or just keep it for your records.

Designed for Different Learners

Support is part of the design, not an afterthought

🧠

Students with IEPs

The platform tracks which supports were used on each question. Your child's report reflects both what they knew and how they worked through harder questions.

🗣️

Language processing differences

Questions are written in plain, direct language. Longer questions can be heard rather than read. Hints restate the problem in simpler terms.

⚡

Attention and focus

One question at a time. No scrolling. No timers. Clean, minimal interface with nothing to distract from the question itself.

📚

Intellectual disability

Level 1 questions reflect real functional ability at a supported level. The goal is to find what a student CAN do — not to document what they can't.
