The Problem

Traditional tests measure the wrong things

Schools have spent decades measuring students against a narrow set of academic benchmarks — right or wrong, above or below grade level. For students who learn differently, this doesn't just miss the mark. It misses the student entirely.

Ripa Elevate was built to change that — by two people who watched it fail from inside the system.

The Level System

Levels, not grades. Growth, not comparison.

Students are never ranked against each other. Each level describes how independently they can think and function.

01
Supported
Demonstrates understanding with structured support and guided steps.
Approx. Gr. 3–5 functional range
02
Developing
Works through problems with occasional hints — building toward independence.
Approx. Gr. 5–7 functional range
03
Independent
Navigates most tasks without support, demonstrating reliable reasoning.
Approx. Gr. 7–9 functional range
04
Advanced
Applies skills flexibly across novel contexts with self-directed thinking.
Gr. 9+ functional range
